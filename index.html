About Us

A.A. Global Consultants Limited is a Lagos-based professional services firm registered with Nigeria's Corporate Affairs Commission, specializing in strategic management consulting, healthcare support services, and integrated business solutions.

Founded on principles of professional excellence and client-centric service delivery, we provide comprehensive strategic, operational, administrative, and business support services to organizations in both public and private sectors.

Our expertise spans management consultancy, healthcare coordination, procurement optimization, and business support services. We partner with healthcare institutions, non-governmental organizations, and government agencies to deliver measurable results and sustainable growth.

Based in Ikeja, Lagos, at Allen Avenue (3rd Floor, Oshopey Plaza), we are committed to excellence and dedicated to helping our clients navigate complex business challenges with professional insight and strategic acumen.
